Step by Step Care Guide

Yniphora Tortoises

Enclosure: Secure, predator-proof outdoor area with shaded hides.
Temperature: 25–32 °C with basking up to 35 °C.
Diet: Grasses, greens, browse; occasional fruit only.
Water: Fresh dish and occasional soaking.
Note: Critically endangered (CITES I). Legal, captive-bred animals only with permits.

Radiated Tortoise

Enclosure: Secure pen with grass, shade, and room to roam.
Temperature: 24–29 °C (ambient), basking 32–35 °C. Provide UVB.
Diet: High-fiber grasses, weeds, leafy greens; limited fruit.
Water/Humidity: Shallow dish, ~50–60% humidity; soak juveniles often.
Note: Critically endangered (CITES I). Only captive-bred with valid paperwork.

Aldabra Tortoise

Enclosure: Large outdoor space; juveniles can start in a 4×2 ft indoor pen.
Temperature: 24–30 °C (ambient), basking 32–35 °C. Provide UVB.
Diet: Mainly grasses, leafy greens, and weeds. Add calcium 2–3×/week.
Water/Humidity: Shallow water dish; soak young tortoises regularly.
Note: Grows very large and can live for decades. Buy captive-bred only.
