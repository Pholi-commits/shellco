About this Tortoise

Radiated tortoises are native to southern Madagascar and are famous for their stunning star-patterned shells. They are among the most beautiful tortoise species in the world, with a lifespan of over 50 years and the ability to grow up to 40 cm in length.

Our captive-bred Radiated tortoises are available in hatchling, 6-month-old, and yearling sizes. They are raised on a nutritious diet of leafy greens, grasses, and cactus pads, promoting strong shells and healthy growth.

Radiated tortoises are calm and intelligent creatures. They thrive in warm, dry environments and require access to UVB light for proper shell and bone development. They are social animals that can interact well with other tortoises in appropriate conditions.

We ship our tortoises overnight in insulated, temperature-controlled containers via UPS or FedEx to guarantee live arrival. Your Radiated tortoise will arrive healthy and ready to start a long, happy life with you.
