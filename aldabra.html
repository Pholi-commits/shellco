About this Tortoise

Aldabra tortoises are one of the largest tortoise species in the world, native to the Aldabra Atoll in the Seychelles. They can weigh over 250 kg as adults and live for more than 100 years, making them extraordinary long-term companions.

Our captive-bred Aldabra tortoises are available in hatchling, 6-month-old, and yearling sizes. They are raised on a carefully balanced diet of calcium-rich greens, Mazuri tortoise chow, and opuntia cactus to ensure healthy growth and strong shells. They are known for their gentle and calm temperament, making them suitable pets for dedicated owners.

Aldabra tortoises require a warm environment and access to UVB light for proper shell and bone development. They are highly resilient, intelligent, and social animals, often thriving in small groups with proper care.

We ship our tortoises overnight via UPS or FedEx in insulated, temperature-controlled containers to guarantee live arrival. Your Aldabra tortoise will arrive safe, healthy, and ready to start a long, happy life with you.
